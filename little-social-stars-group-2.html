About the Program

Two Levels
So every child keeps growing — never repeating
Children move through two levels within Little Social Stars across their years with us.
1

Level 1 — Foundations

New children · Ages 3–4 · Year One

Predictable routines and transitions — feeling safe and knowing what comes next
Identify and name 5 core feelings — happy, sad, scared, angry, excited
Body signals — understanding what feelings feel like in the body
Build a personal toolkit of 2–3 calming strategies
The 4 friendship skills — getting attention, joining play, sharing ideas, including others
Saying sorry and beginning to repair after conflict
Introduction to 4 problem-solving solutions — trade, wait, find another, walk away
Kinder-Ready skills — lining up, raising a hand, waiting, asking for help
2

Level 2 — Building & Kinder-Ready

Returning children · Ages 4–5 · Year Two

Expand feelings bank — frustrated, embarrassed, proud, nervous, overwhelmed
Identify personal triggers — what changes my zone and why?
Build a personalised toolkit of 5–6 calming strategies
Self-advocacy — "I'm in yellow, I need a moment to calm down"
Complex friendship — compromise, exclusion, staying kind under pressure
Noticing and supporting a friend's feelings
Expand solutions bank from 4 to 9 — adding compromise, talking it out, repair and more
All skills integrated — Kinder-Ready graduation celebration
🎓  Children completing Level 2 receive a  Kinder-Ready Social Stars certificate  and are celebrated before moving up.
