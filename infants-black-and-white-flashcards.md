## Our collection of Black & White cards

During the initial three months of a baby's life, visual development undergoes significant changes. It is crucial to actively promote and support this developmental process by providing a diverse range of visual stimulation. This approach recognizes the unique and evolving nature of a baby's visual abilities during this early stage, emphasizing the importance of tailored activities and stimuli to foster healthy visual development.
