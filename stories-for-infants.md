## What research speaks about Visual Stimulation?

Researchers have repeatedly shown that newborns prefer to look at black and white geometric shapes, rather than bright colors or pastels. It is proved by researchers that high contrast colours, such as black and white are part of a child's vision capabilities. They are able to track contrasting colours due to the stimulation it brings.
