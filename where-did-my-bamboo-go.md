Where did my bamboo go?  
Rs. 549

Where did my bamboo go? story kit consists of black and white story flashcards, a recorded audio of the story and an audio-video version of the story. ‘Where did my bamboo go?' is the story that introduces two concepts – Opposite words - Yes & No and Wild animals. This story has been developed with high contrasting black and white images designed with clearly-defined patterns to provide optimal visual stimulation to babies. The dialogues written on each spread describes the scene supporting the characters. The audio-video version adds to the utility of the product as the story can be played on a tablet or a laptop and will simply engage the infant stimulating its visual and auditory organs. The recorded audio of the story can be used in the absence of the adult while the infant explores the story cards.

The infant learns to build the association of sound and vision together stimulating all the sensory organs of his body every time the story session takes place.
